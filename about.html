About

NewBatteries

An early-stage Australian platform helping people identify replacement batteries and connect with suitable suppliers — without the guesswork.

What is NewBatteries?

NewBatteries is a prototype battery identification and supplier-connection platform built for the Australian market. It addresses a common frustration: knowing you need a replacement battery, but not knowing which one — or where to get it.

Batteries come in hundreds of formats across cars, motorcycles, marine vessels, caravans, power tools, solar systems, mobility aids, and more. Getting the wrong one wastes time and money, and can damage expensive equipment.

What NewBatteries does

  • Guides you through identifying the correct replacement battery using one clear question at a time.
  • Accepts multiple identification pathways: battery number, specifications, photo upload, or description-based help.
  • Connects enquiries with local battery retailers, specialist suppliers, and mobile replacement services.
  • Covers consumer, commercial, agricultural, and industrial battery categories.

What NewBatteries does not do

  • NewBatteries does not sell batteries directly.
  • NewBatteries cannot guarantee that any suggested battery is compatible — compatibility must always be independently verified before purchase or installation.
  • NewBatteries is not a licensed electrician, automotive technician, or battery specialist. Guidance provided is informational only.

Current stage

This is an early-stage prototype. Features are limited, supplier coverage is not yet established across all regions, and the platform is under active development. Enquiries submitted through the finder are reviewed manually at this stage.

Australian-first

NewBatteries is built specifically for the Australian market, using Australian battery standards, terminology, and supplier networks. Australian battery sizes, group numbers, and chemistry types are all considered in the identification flow.

Contact

NewBatteries is an early-stage project. For enquiries about the platform, supplier participation, or feedback, use the contact information on the For Suppliers page.
