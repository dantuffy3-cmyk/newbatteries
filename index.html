Frequently asked questions

Does NewBatteries sell batteries directly?

No. This prototype focuses on identification and supplier connection.

What if I do not know my battery number?

You can provide equipment details, submit specs, or upload a battery photo.

Can you guarantee a battery is compatible?

No. Compatibility must always be independently verified before purchase or installation.

Can I request installation?

Yes. You can indicate supply and installation, mobile replacement, or identification help.

What happens to my old battery?

You can request old battery recycling information in your enquiry.

Is the service available across Australia?

This is an early-stage platform and coverage may vary while participation grows.
