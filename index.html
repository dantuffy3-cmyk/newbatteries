Frequently asked questions

Does NewBatteries sell batteries directly?

No. NewBatteries focuses on helping people identify the battery they need and connecting them with suitable suppliers or installers.

What if I do not know my battery number?

That's fine. You can describe your equipment, enter specifications if known, or indicate you have photos. The finder guides you through whichever path suits your situation.

Can you guarantee a battery is compatible?

No. Compatibility must always be independently verified before purchase or installation, especially for critical or high-voltage systems. NewBatteries helps identify and connect — it does not replace expert advice.

Can I request installation?

Yes. The finder lets you indicate whether you need supply only, supply and installation, mobile replacement, or just identification help.

What happens to my old battery?

You can request old battery recycling information as part of your enquiry. Disposal requirements vary and should follow local regulations and safety guidance.

Is the service available across Australia?

This is an early-stage platform. Coverage may vary while supplier participation is being established.
