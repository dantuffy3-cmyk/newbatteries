    Enter the battery number

    The battery number or part number is printed on a label on the battery itself.

    Where to find it: Look for a code on the top or side label of your battery. Common formats include DIN66MF, NS70, N70ZZ, LN2, or a manufacturer part number.
    e.g. Century, Exide, SuperCharge, Optima, Odyssey
    Enter exactly as it appears on the label
    Please enter the battery model or part number, or tick to skip.

    Common Australian formats include:

    • DIN/BCI numbers — e.g. DIN66MF, N70ZZ, NS40ZL
    • Group numbers — e.g. 24, 27, 31, 34/78
    • LN codes — e.g. LN0, LN2, LN3, LN4 (used in modern European vehicles)
    • Manufacturer codes — vary by brand; enter exactly as printed
