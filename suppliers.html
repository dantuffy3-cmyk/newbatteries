For suppliers & installers

Join the NewBatteries network.

NewBatteries is building an Australian supplier network connecting battery retailers, specialists, and mobile replacement services with customers who need them.

Who we are looking for

NewBatteries is inviting expressions of interest from:

  • Battery retailers and wholesalers with retail or trade supply capability
  • Specialist battery shops with identification and testing expertise
  • Auto electricians and automotive workshops that supply and fit batteries
  • Mobile battery replacement services operating in regional or metro areas
  • Industrial, marine, and renewable energy battery suppliers
  • Mobility aid and medical equipment battery specialists

How the platform works for suppliers

Customers submit battery enquiries through the NewBatteries finder. Enquiries include:

  • Equipment category and description
  • Battery identification details (number, specs, or photo)
  • Type of help required (supply only, supply and fit, mobile, identification)
  • Location (suburb, postcode, state)
  • Customer contact details

Matched enquiries are forwarded to relevant suppliers based on location, category, and capability. Suppliers respond directly to customers.

Current stage

This is an early-stage prototype. Supplier matching is handled manually while the platform is developed. Registered suppliers will be among the first to be connected with enquiries as the network launches.

Register interest

To register your interest as a supplier or installer, email us with:

  • Your business name, location, and coverage area
  • The battery categories you supply or service
  • Whether you offer mobile, in-store, or trade supply services
Note: This is an early-stage platform. No formal supplier agreement exists yet. Registering interest does not create any obligation on either party.
